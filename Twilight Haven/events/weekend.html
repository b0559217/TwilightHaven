Events hosted by Twilight Haven!

  • We have theme nights! We have a variety of delicious themed dinner nights here at Twilight Haven! From Argentinian Grill night, a Jazz Club night and a Taste of Italy night. We even have a murder mystery nights for our guests to be able to interact and try out new experiences!
  • We also offer a photography tour! We take you on a tour of the beautiful breathtaking sights of nature there is to see around Twilight Haven! Guests enjoy having these pictures to show off later after their trip!
  • We also offer our fitness classes such as HITT classes, Yoga classes, and Kickboxing classes for our guest!
  • At Twilight Haven we know you are here for a good time! Sometimes that means you don't always have the time to stop and eat something when exploring a new place! That's why we got you covered! Guests are welcome to order a packed lunch to buy the day before their day trips! Available everyday of the week.
