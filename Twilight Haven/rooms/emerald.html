About our Emerald Room

Welcome to our Emerald Haven Suite, a luxurious retreat designed to indulge your senses. This room costs 275 US dollars per night. Picture yourself entering a room where deep emerald green walls are tastefully accented with elegant gold trim, creating a regal yet inviting atmosphere.

A plush, oversized bed dressed in crisp white and emerald bedding beckons you to unwind, while a tufted velvet sofa provides the perfect nook to curl up with a book or a cup of tea.

The heart of the room is a cozy fireplace with an ornate gold frame, casting a warm glow across the space. In front of the fireplace, you’ll find a beautifully arranged gold tray laden with delicious treats—think artisanal pastries and hand-selected chocolates, just for you.

Above, a grand chandelier sparkles, enhancing the room's warm ambiance. Ornate gold mirrors reflect the soft lighting, while lush potted plants add a touch of nature. Floor-to-ceiling emerald and gold curtains frame a large window that bathes the room in natural light during the day, making it as soothing as it is opulent.

Whether you're here for a romantic escape or a restful getaway, the Emerald Haven Suite promises comfort, elegance, and unforgettable charm. We can't wait to welcome you!
