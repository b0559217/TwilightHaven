About our Sapphire Room

Welcome to our luxurious and serene bed and breakfast, where comfort meets elegance in every detail. Step into our signature Sapphire Suite, a haven of relaxation wrapped in deep, calming hues of sapphire blue.This room cost is 250 US dollars per night.

The room is designed with indulgence in mind: a plush, king-sized bed adorned with velvet sapphire bedding, inviting you to unwind after a day of exploration.

Bask in the warmth of your own private fireplace, casting a cozy glow across the room as you sink into one of the comfortable velvet armchairs. Above the bed, a stunning image of a sapphire gem adds a unique touch of elegance.

Natural light floods through large windows draped with luxurious sapphire curtains, creating the perfect balance of cozy warmth and refreshing brightness.

Golden accents throughout the room, from picture frames to bedside tables, bring a subtle touch of sophistication. Whether you're relaxing by the fire, enjoying the peaceful ambiance, or admiring the thoughtful design, this room promises an unforgettable stay.

If you're looking for a retreat that combines luxury, comfort, and beauty, our Sapphire Suite awaits you!
